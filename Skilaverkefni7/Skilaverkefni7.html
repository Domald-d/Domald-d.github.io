Gameplay

Diabo II follows the story of a hero who has set out to defeat the Prime evils who have risen up from hell and made their way into the world of sanctuary and are wreacking havoc and destruction the game takes place in 5 acts or 5 towns and it's upto the hero to vanquish the evil minions of hell and restore order to sanctuary, players must defeat the three Prime evils who are brothers and are dubbed as the following, Mephisto the lord of Hatred, Diablo the lord of Terror and Baal the lord of Destruction. these brothers are scattered around the world of sanctuary and it falls upon the hero to end their reign of terror, the game takes place in 5 acts or 5 towns each act has a unique setting be it in act 2 where you are in a beautiful desert climate or in act 3 which is a forest swamp infested with all kind of critters
